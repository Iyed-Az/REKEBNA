How It Works

Our carpooling platform"REKEBNA" offers a convenient way to share rides and reduce commuting costs. Simply sign up, create your profile, and either search for available rides or offer a ride yourself. Once you find a suitable match, book your seat or accept passengers for your journey. Coordinate details with your fellow carpoolers through our messaging system, meet at the designated spot, and enjoy the ride together. Afterward, rate your experience to help maintain a trusted community. Join us today to make your commute more affordable and eco-friendly!
